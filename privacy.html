Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide when creating an account including business name, email address, and password. We also collect usage data such as missed call logs, SMS records, and appointment bookings generated through our platform.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to provide and improve our services, process payments, send service notifications, and respond to customer support requests. We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

3. SMS and Phone Data

CallZap processes phone numbers and SMS messages as part of our core service. This data is used solely to provide missed call text back functionality. We comply with all applicable telecommunications laws including TCPA guidelines.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

5. Third Party Services

We use trusted third-party services including Twilio for SMS delivery and Paddle for payment processing. These services have their own privacy policies and we encourage you to review them.

6. Cookies

We use cookies to maintain your login session and improve user experience. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but this may affect functionality.

7. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. Upon account deletion, your data will be removed within 30 days.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data at any time. Contact us at getnevermissai@gmail.com to exercise these rights.

9. Children's Privacy

CallZap is not intended for use by children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
